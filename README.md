# 📊 ShopNest Store Performance Analysis Project | Power BI

## 📝 Project Description
This Power BI capstone project analyzes the **ShopNest Store dataset** to uncover insights into sales performance, product ratings, customer geography, payment behavior, and delivery efficiency.  
The goal was to design a **single-page interactive dashboard** that answers eight key business questions using **ETL workflows, star schema modeling, calculated columns, DAX measures, and visual storytelling**.  

---

## 🔎 Overview
- Built a **star schema data model** across 9 datasets (orders, items, payments, reviews, products, customers, sellers, geolocation, category translation).  
- Applied **ETL workflows**: data cleaning, null handling, transformations, and relationship building.  
- Designed **calculated columns** (Order Year, Month, Quarter, Delivery Status, Delay Status).  
- Implemented **DAX measures** for KPIs like Total Sales, Delayed Orders, On-Time Orders, Average Ratings, Payment Counts.  
- Delivered a **single-page interactive dashboard** answering 8 business-driven questions.  

---

## ✨ Key Features
- **Sales Insights**: Top categories, seasonal peaks, yearly revenue growth.  
- **Delivery Performance**: Delayed vs on-time orders, category-specific delay analysis.  
- **Customer Geography**: State-wise sales distribution via treemap visualization.  
- **Payment Behavior**: Dominance of credit card transactions (74%).  
- **Product Ratings**: Top 10 vs Bottom 10 categories for customer satisfaction.  
- **Interactive Filters**: Product category, customer state, order month/quarter/year.  
- **Visual Storytelling**: Bar charts, line charts, donut chart, treemap, waterfall chart.  

---

## 🛠️ Technology Used
- **Power BI**: Dashboard design, DAX measures, Power Query ETL.  
- **Data Modeling**: Star schema with fact and dimension tables.  
- **Data Sources**: 9 CSV datasets (orders, items, payments, reviews, products, customers, sellers, geolocation, category translation).  
- **ETL Techniques**: Null replacement, column renaming, calculated columns, relationship building.  

---

## 📈 Business Insights
- Categories like **health_beauty** and **watches_accessories** dominate sales.  
- High delays in **watches, toys, telephony** categories suggest fulfillment issues.  
- Seasonal peaks in **Q2** and strong revenue growth from ₹6.2M (2016) to ₹13.6M (2018).  
- **SP state** leads with ₹5.2M in sales, followed by RJ and MG.  
- Credit card is the **preferred payment method** (74% of transactions).  
- Top-rated categories (fixed_telephony, housewares) can be promoted for customer satisfaction.
